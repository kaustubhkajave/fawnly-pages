Fawnly Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Fawnly is a local-first walking and step tracking app. It helps you view your movement, estimate distance and calories, and set gentle walking preferences.

Data Stored on Your Device

Fawnly stores app data locally on your device. This may include:

  • Step history
  • Daily walking goal
  • Height and weight settings used for estimates
  • Distance unit preference
  • Theme preference
  • Daily summary notification preference
  • Onboarding and other app settings

This data is used to make the app work on your device. Fawnly v1 does not sync this data to a Fawnly server.

Accounts and Cloud Sync

Fawnly v1 does not create user accounts and does not include cloud sync. Your activity history and settings stay on your device unless you choose to back up, transfer, or share your device data using operating system features outside of Fawnly.

Ads, Analytics, and Tracking

Fawnly v1 does not include ads, analytics, or third-party tracking. Fawnly does not sell your data.

Health Data and Device Sensors

Fawnly uses device step and motion features to show step activity when available and permitted. Fawnly v1 does not use Apple HealthKit or Android Health Connect.

On Android, Fawnly v1 can count live steps while the app is open. It does not provide full background step history through Health Connect.

Notifications

Daily Summary notifications are optional. If enabled, Fawnly schedules a local reminder with generic copy, such as a prompt to check your summary. The notification does not include exact step counts, calories, or distance in v1.

You can turn Daily Summary notifications off in the app settings or manage notification permission in your device settings.

Estimates

Distance and calorie values are estimates. They may use your step count, height, weight, stride settings, and unit preferences. These estimates are provided for general wellness context only.

Not Medical Advice

Fawnly is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you have health concerns or questions about physical activity, talk with a qualified healthcare professional.

Deleting Your Data

Because Fawnly stores v1 data locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting Fawnly from your device or clearing the app's stored data through your device settings where supported.
